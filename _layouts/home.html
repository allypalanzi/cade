Cade Robinson

Ever since I can remember I have been finding makeshift ways of applying my art onto different surfaces. From making stickers with address labels when I was 8 to hand-painting type onto t-shirts when I was 16. After recieving my BFA in Graphic Design it became clear that Washington DC was not the place for me to pursue my dream career in screen printing due to the lack of shops, let alone open positions. When my partner and I moved our butts accross the country in search of happiness and belonging, I came to appreciate Portland’s love of craftsmanship and close ties to traditional mediums.

I spend most of my days with my partner who is a fellow graphic designer, Dobby the awkward dachshund, Swoon the sweet rat, and Knives the scrappy kitten.
